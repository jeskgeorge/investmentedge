The Power of Compounding in Mutual Funds

Published on Jan 10, 2026 ‚Ä¢ 6 Minute Read

In the world of investing, there is a concept often called the "eighth wonder of the world": Compounding. At InvestmentEdge.in, we emphasize that wealth isn't just about how much you invest, but how long you let it grow.

What is Compounding?

Compounding is the process where your investment's earnings are reinvested to generate additional earnings over time. This growth is exponential rather than linear.

Key Insight: In a Mutual Fund SIP (Systematic Investment Plan), your small monthly contributions benefit from compounding, turning modest savings into a substantial corpus over 15-20 years.

The biggest ally of an investor is time. Even a delay of 5 years in starting your investment journey can result in a significant difference in the final maturity amount. This is why we help our clients open their demat accounts and start their journeys as early as possible.
